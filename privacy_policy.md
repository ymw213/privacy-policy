# Privacy Policy for Limëtr

**Last updated: April 6, 2026**

## Overview

Limëtr ("the app") is a light metering application developed by Dulu Studio. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

## Camera Permission

The app requires access to your device's camera solely to:
- Display a live camera preview for exposure metering
- Analyze scene brightness and color temperature (white balance) locally on your device

**The camera feed is processed entirely on your device. No images or video are captured, stored, or transmitted.**

## Data Collection

Limëtr does **not**:
- Collect any personal information
- Store images or video
- Transmit any data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Require an account or registration

The only data stored locally on your device is your selected metering mode preference (e.g., Program, Av, Tv), which is saved using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

Limëtr does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
